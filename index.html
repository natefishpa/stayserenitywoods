The Property

A retreat built around the outdoors.

This is not a sterile hotel-box vacation. It is a woods-and-water getaway where the house gives you space to gather, sleep, cook, and reset — while the land does the heavy lifting.

Spend the day hiking, fishing, exploring the Finger Lakes region, relaxing on the deck, swimming, or sitting near the fire pit. Then fall asleep knowing tomorrow can be as lazy or adventurous as you want it to be.

Guest Guide

Peaceful, private, and rural — with a few country rules.

This is a true rural property, so guests should expect nature, wildlife, uneven terrain, and quiet-hour expectations. The goal is simple: enjoy the land, respect the neighbors, and keep the sanctuary peaceful for everyone.

Respect property lines

Stay on the Serenity Woods property and do not wander onto neighboring land.

Quiet outside after 11 PM

Sound travels in the valley. Late-night fun belongs inside.

Use caution outdoors

There are woods, slopes, wildlife, ticks, and natural hazards. Supervise children closely.

Pool and fire safety

Follow posted instructions for the pool, fire pit, trash, and checkout basics.
